Fractal Formula Generator

Automatically generate Fractal Formulas by entering an ID Number.

Click one of the buttons to generate a Quadratic (0-255), Cubic (0-16383), Quartic (0-131071), or Quintic (0-1048576) ABS Mandelbrot formula.

The program supports syntax for Java, JS, C, C++, and GLSL.
